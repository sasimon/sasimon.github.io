Oatmeal Raisin Bread

by Clazarus

A simple and egg-free recipe. Best served cold. Makes one loaf, but often doubled.

Rate/Review | Review Page

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup whole-wheat flour
  • 1 cup baking soda
  • 1/4 cup baking powder
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 tsp molasses
  • 2 1/2 cups plain yogurt
  • 1 cup raisins

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350° F (230° C).
  2. Add all ingredients together up to and including rolled oats in a lage bowl.
  3. On the side, mix molasses and yogurt until evenly distributed.
  4. Add mixture to bowl and stir until smooth.
  5. Stir in raisins.
  6. Grease or spray 12 x 6 in. pan. Add mixture to pan.
  7. Bake until golden brown.
  8. Best served when having been refrigerated for at least one day.

Reviews

I was interested to find this egg-free recipe when I was five years old. My mother, sister, and I have spent a lifetime of making it together. The recipe is simple enough that we have it all committed to memory. The wondrous taste of chewy oats and rasins in our mouths has proven to be worth more than the work involved in making it!

— Reviewed on Sep. 22, 2014 by SSIMON6.
